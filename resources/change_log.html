Version: 1.0.0-beta

  • Mod: Initial release. For Minecraft 1.7.10, Forge 10.13.2.1230, and JeckelCoreLibrary 0.5.1-beta.
  • New: Admin Fluid Provider. Given a valid fluid container item as a template, the block will provide a maximum of 32 buckets of that fluid to every request it receives.
  • New: Admin RF Provider. Provides a maximum of 32,000 RF units to every request it receives. Additionally, will inject up to 32,000 RF units into any RF receiver in one of the 6 adjacent tiles.
  • New: Admin EU Provider. Provides a maximum of 32,000 EU (IC2 Energy Units) to every request it receives.
